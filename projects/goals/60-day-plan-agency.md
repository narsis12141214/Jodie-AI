---

## Constraint Acknowledgements (read before the plan)

**Outreach reality:** Zizi is one person. 100/day is not a day-1 target — it is a day-60 ambition. The plan stages this honestly: 40/day by day 20, 70/day by day 45, 100/day by day 60. Hitting 100/day requires a decision at the day 20 checkpoint: partial automation, a second person, or a revised target. That decision cannot be deferred.

**Stretch vs floor:** The plan is built to hit floor targets reliably. Stretch targets are flagged explicitly. Do not optimise for stretch at the expense of floor.

**Platform sequencing:** Instagram launches now. LinkedIn launches week 3 (minimum). YouTube and Facebook do not launch until Instagram has 3 weeks of consistent rhythm and at least one client is closed. Spreading across all platforms before one is working is the fastest way to do all of them badly.

**Revenue sequencing:** Close before you build. The only exception is demos — demos are built to close. Full client builds do not start without payment.

**Risk 1: Warm pipeline doesn't convert in phase 1**
Mitigation: Paris, Maria, Hasan, Walid are the known warm leads. If all four decline or go cold by day 14, Hadi goes direct to personal network contacts — anyone who knows him and runs a business. The first client will almost certainly come from a warm connection, not cold outreach. Do not wait for cold outreach to produce a close.

**Risk 2: Automation build takes longer than Day 10**
Mitigation: Decision confirmed 1 April. N8N automation for lead identification and Step 1 opener generation builds week 1. If build runs over, Zizi operates manually at current capacity (20-30/day) until automation is live — outreach does not pause. The 100/day target still requires a Day 20 checkpoint decision on whether a second person is needed.

**Risk 3: Content doesn't get filmed**
Mitigation: 5 Reels are currently unfilmed. This is week 1's creative priority. Without filmed content, the Instagram strategy collapses to static posts — which works short-term but limits reach. Hadi must film week 1. Jodie can schedule and caption everything else.
