# 30 London Locations in 30 Days — @hadyyazdani

**Format:** Daily reel, 15-20 seconds each, Hadi to camera at the location
**Filming:** Can batch-film 5-6 locations per day across 5-6 filming sessions
**Posting:** One reel per day for 30 consecutive days
**Series hook:** "Day [X] of 30 London locations every couple should know about."
**Pillar:** Education (primary) + My Story (secondary)
**Goal:** Position Hadi as THE London location authority. High save rate. Discoverable via location tags and hashtags.

---

## How to film

- Vertical (9:16), phone or camera
- Stand at the location, face to camera
- Open every reel with: "Day [X]. [Location name]."
- Deliver the script naturally, not word for word
- Show the location behind you or cut to B-roll of the spot
- 15-20 seconds maximum. Short beats long.
- Text overlay on screen: location name + "Day X of 30"

---

## Batching strategy

Film in geographic clusters to save travel time:

**Batch 1 (Central/Westminster):** Days 1-6
