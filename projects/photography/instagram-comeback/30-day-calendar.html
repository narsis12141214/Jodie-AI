Blog repurpose — London Wedding Photographer Guide
Carousel Education B
Slides (7)
  • How to choose the right wedding photographer in London — from a photographer:
  • Look at their full galleries, not just their highlights. Every photographer has 10 great shots. You need to know what the rest looks like.
  • Meet them before you book. If the conversation feels awkward, the shoot will too.
  • Check how they handle low light and rain. London will give you both.
  • Ask about their approach to nervous couples. The answer tells you everything.
  • Price is not the metric. Ask to see a full wedding day gallery at whatever price point you're considering.
  • The right photographer is the one you forget is there. That is what you are looking for.

Carousel Education A
Slides (7)
  • What makes a couples session in London actually work:
  • Location second, connection first. The best images come from couples who are present with each other, not performing for a camera.
  • Wear what you would wear on a good date. Not what you think looks good in photos.
