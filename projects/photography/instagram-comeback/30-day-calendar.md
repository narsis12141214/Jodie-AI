**Caption A** = Natural voice. 3-5 lines. Emotional, restrained, specific. No hard CTA. (@henrysdiary style)

- Caption A: measure saves and comments (emotional connection)
- Caption B: measure DMs and profile visits (conversion)
- Winner gets 70% of posts in month 2
