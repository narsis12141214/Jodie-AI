The Problem

Four things costing your gym members and revenue every week

You are not losing members because your training is bad. You are losing them because the experience outside the session is not keeping up. Here is where it happens.

📞

Enquiries that go unanswered

Someone finds your gym on Google at 9pm. They call or message asking about membership options. Nobody answers because the studio closed at 8. By morning they have joined the gym that replied at 9:05pm.

67% of gym enquiries happen outside business hours
🪑

Class no-shows that waste your capacity

A member booked a class last week. No reminder was sent. They forgot. The spot sat empty while your waitlist had three people on it. Each no-show is a missed opportunity to serve someone who actually wanted to be there.

Average: 15-25% class no-show rate
🚪

Members who quietly disappear

A member came three times a week for two months. Then twice. Then once. Then not at all. Nobody noticed until the direct debit cancelled. No check-in message. No re-engagement offer. They are now at the gym down the road.

50% of members who leave were recoverable
📱

Social media that goes dark between launches

You post when a new programme launches. Then nothing for three weeks. New members searching for gyms in your area find the one that posts transformation stories every day, not the one that posted last month.

Consistency beats quality on social
