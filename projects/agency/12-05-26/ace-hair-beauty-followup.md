# Ace Hair & Beauty Salon — Follow-up Sequence

**Date:** 12 May 2026
**Status:** Verbal yes on Friday 8 May, contract sent Saturday 9 May, silent since. Zizi called Monday 11 May (owner "in a meeting"). Owner had an open question about Phorest CRM integration that was never directly answered.
**Strategy:** Re-enter with the answer to his question, not a chase. Warm reopener, then close question.
**Channel:** WhatsApp (voice note first, text second). Sent by Hadi, not Zizi.

---

## Step 1 — Voice note (send first, 30 to 40 seconds)

Send via WhatsApp. Read warm and conversational, not transactional. Pause briefly between sentences.

> Hey [name], Hadi here. Got your question about Phorest. Yes, we can integrate, no problem at all. I'll drop you a text right after this with exactly what we need from you. Once we have that, we can get you signed and start the build this week. Are we good to move forward?

**Notes on delivery:**
- Use his first name
- Sound like a friend who solved a problem for him, not a salesperson chasing a contract
- Do not apologise for the gap
- Do not bring up the silence

---

## Step 2 — Text message (send within 60 seconds of the voice note)

Send via WhatsApp text, immediately after the voice note lands.

> Hi [name], following up from the voice note.
>
> Phorest integration confirmed. To set it up, we need two things from you:
>
> 1. Your salon's business_id and API key from Phorest
> 2. Your service and staff catalogue: list of services, durations, prices, staff members, and which staff perform which services
>
> Once we have those, bookings made by the voice agent sync straight back into your Phorest account, no double-entry needed.
>
> Send those across and we'll get the build moving.

**Notes:**
- The text delivers the technical info he asked for
- It does NOT repeat the closing question (the voice note carries that)
- It ends on forward motion ("get the build moving"), not a question, so we are not double-pushing

---

## After sending

- Silent for 48 hours from Hadi and Zizi
- If he replies with the integration details: send the contract link with a short confirming message, ask for signature within 24 hours
- If he replies with a question or objection: handle it directly, do not push for the close in the same message
- If he goes silent again past 48 hours: escalate to a final in-person walk-in during his quiet hours (the salon is physical, this is leverage we have)
- Log all responses to projects/agency/pipeline immediately

## Why this sequence works

The owner had an open loop (Phorest question) that was never closed. His silence may not have been pure cold feet. It may have been "I'll wait until they answer my question before I sign". Re-entering with the answer reopens the door warmly without re-selling the package. The voice note is the personal nudge. The text is the substance. Both are short.
