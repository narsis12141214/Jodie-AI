## Priority 1 — Industry Landing Pages (Week 1-2)

Build 4 dedicated pages, one per vertical:
- clickaiagency.com/restaurants
- clickaiagency.com/dental
- clickaiagency.com/beauty
- clickaiagency.com/fitness

Each page:
- Headline: "[Industry] owners lose [X] because they [pain point]. AI fixes it in [timeframe]."
- Copy written in real owner language, not tech language
- Industry-specific pain points from the ICA
- Social proof (case studies when available, demo results for now)
- CTA: "Free 15-minute automation audit for [industry]"
- Links to Click Pro as the recommended package

## Priority 2 — Referral Partnerships (Week 2-3)

Target: 50 accountants, business consultants, and marketing agencies in London
- Use LinkedIn + Google Maps to build list
- Commission: 15-20% of first-year revenue per referred client
- Simple 3-line DM template
- Track in spreadsheet
- Provide each partner a referral link + one-paragraph email template for their clients

## Priority 3 — Case Studies (Before month-end)

Publish 2 case studies:
- Randeszvous (if closed) or Ahla Tallah / Dr Sara demos
- Format: Before/After, time saved, revenue impact
- Publish on clickaiagency.com/case-studies + LinkedIn

## Priority 4 — LinkedIn 2x per week (Ongoing)

- Monday: Industry insight with a specific number
- Thursday: Behind-the-scenes win or build
- Polls, short video clips, case snippets
- 30 mins per week effort

## Priority 5 — Directory Listings (One afternoon)

- G2, Capterra, Futurepedia, Product Hunt
