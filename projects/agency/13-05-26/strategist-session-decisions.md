## TOPIC 1 — Click Voice tier (LOCKED)

A new entry-level tier introduced to give restaurants a lower-friction yes. Designed as a LAND product, not a destination product. Goal: upgrade Click Voice clients to Click+ within 60-90 days.

### Final spec

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | Click Voice (single tier, v1) |
| Target | Restaurants only, v1. Expand after 10 closes. |
| Monthly fee | £290/month |
| Setup (standard) | £350 |
| Setup (launch pricing) | £100 (71% off, "Founding Cohort Launch Pricing", no public quantification) |
| Internal cohort target | 20 closes across all tiers, then revisit |
| Minutes | 1,000/month combined voice + transactional confirmations |
| Overage | £0.30/minute |
| Trial | 14-day trial begins at go-live. No money-back guarantee. £100 setup paid at signing. |
| Includes | AI voice agent (custom-built), Click Desk AI dashboard, basic Google Business Profile setup, transactional confirmations and reminders via WhatsApp, SMS, or email |
| Excludes | Social media, content production, website, SEO beyond GBP, full WhatsApp automation, deep CRM integrations |
| Upgrade path | Day 60 soft pitch to Click+. 50% off Click+ first month if upgrading within 90 days. |

### Key rationales

- **1,000 minutes (same as Click+):** Hadi's bottom-up math (10-15 calls/day × 50% AI handling × 3-5 min per call = 750-1,050 min/month) showed 400-500 min was unrealistic. Matching Click+'s minute allocation makes the upgrade conversation about features, not capacity.
- **£100 launch setup (not waived):** Skin in the game filters tire-kickers. Cultural concern: refund-based offers exploited in target market. Setup commitment removes that risk.
- **Dashboard included:** UX argument trumped cannibalisation fence argument. Restaurant owners need 2-tap booking management and agent pause control.
- **14-day trial (not 7, not 30-day MBG):** Prospects say 7 days isn't enough. 30-day money-back guarantee creates cultural refund-exploitation risk in target market. 14-day trial with non-refundable setup is the cleanest solution.

### Positioning

"Done-for-you AI voice operations for your restaurant." Compete against platform AI features (OpenTable, Resy, Phorest incoming features) and doing nothing. NOT against SaaS voice tools (Bland, Synthflow, Air.ai).

### Cannibalisation fences (preventing Click+ downgrades)

- Restaurants only in v1
- No social, no content, no website, no SEO
- No deep CRM integrations
- Same dashboard but restricted feature set
- Day 60 upgrade pitch is planned, not optional

## TOPIC 2 — Positioning shift (LOCKED)

The core insight: we have been selling features (12 posts, voice receptionist, WhatsApp) when buyers care about outcomes (recovered missed calls, hours back, more bookings).

### The shift

| OLD | NEW |
|---|---|
| "AI voice receptionist for small businesses" | "Done-for-you AI Operations Partner for [industry]" |
| Lead with features in conversation | Lead with outcomes in conversation |
| Compare to AI tools | Compare to doing nothing OR hiring staff |
| Features in H1, body, everywhere | SEO-led H1, positioning-led H2, outcome-led body |

### The SEO synthesis

"Done-for-you AI Operations Partner" is brand positioning, not a search query. Nobody Googles it. The resolution:

- **H1 + page title + meta description:** lead with the search query (e.g., "AI Phone Answering for UK Restaurants")
- **H2 + body copy + sales conversations:** lead with the positioning (Done-for-you AI Operations Partner)

Keyword research at `projects/agency/13-05-26/landing-page-keyword-research.md` provides the specific H1/title/meta for 5 landing pages.

### Why this positioning survives the platform threat

OpenTable, Phorest, Toast, SquareUp will add AI receptionists in the next 12-18 months. If we sell "AI voice receptionist", we lose. If we sell "Done-for-you AI Operations team", we win — because they can't replicate done-for-you customisation, speed of iteration, full-stack service depth, and custom voice that sounds like the client.

When a platform launches voice AI, we say: "Yes, OpenTable will have a voice agent. It'll be generic. Ours sounds like you, knows your menu, handles your specials. Plus we run your Instagram, your TikTok, your reviews, your SEO. They give you a feature. We give you a team."
