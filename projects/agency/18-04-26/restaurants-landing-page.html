The consistency problem

Most restaurants post for a week, then go silent for a month. The Instagram algorithm stops showing your content to your followers. New customers searching for places to eat never find you. Your competitors who post three times a week appear in Explore, get saved, get shared, and get the bookings you should have had.

Consistent posting does three things: it keeps your restaurant visible to the people who already follow you, it puts your food and atmosphere in front of new customers through Explore and hashtags, and it builds the kind of social proof that makes someone choose you over the place next door.

With Click AI, your restaurant posts 8 branded pieces of content every week across Instagram and Facebook. Planned monthly, approved by you, published automatically. You never think about it. Your feed never goes dark. Your customers never forget you exist.
