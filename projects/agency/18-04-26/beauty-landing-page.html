The Problem

Four things silently costing your clinic money every week

You are not losing clients because your treatments are not good enough. You are losing them because the admin around the treatments is not keeping up. Here is where it happens.

💬

DMs and enquiries that go unanswered

A potential client sends a DM at 8pm asking about lip filler pricing. You are with a client. By the time you reply the next morning, they have already booked with the clinic that responded in 10 minutes. Most beauty enquiries happen outside working hours. If nobody is answering, someone else is.

68% of beauty enquiries come via DM
🪑

No-shows and last-minute cancellations

A client booked a facial three weeks ago. No reminder was sent. They forgot. Your therapist had a 90-minute gap in the middle of the day. The slot could have been filled from your waitlist if anyone had known in time.

Average: £300-600/month in empty slots

Clients who never rebook

A client had an amazing experience. Loved the results. Told you they would be back. Then nothing. No follow-up message. No rebooking reminder. Three months later they see another clinic on Instagram and book there instead. Not because you did anything wrong. Because nobody followed up.

40% of clients never return without follow-up

Social media that stops and starts

You post transformation photos for a week, then nothing for three weeks. The algorithm stops showing your content. New clients searching for treatments in your area never find you. Your competitors who post consistently appear in Explore and get the enquiries.

76% of clients find clinics via social media
