## REEL 1 — The missed call problem
**Hook:** "Every missed call is a booking your competitor just got."

**FULL SCRIPT:**

0:00–0:03
"Every missed call is a booking your competitor just got."

0:03–0:30
"Most restaurants, clinics, and practices miss between 20 and 40 percent of their calls during busy periods. Not because they don't care. Because one person can't answer the phone and serve a customer at the same time. An AI receptionist answers every call, every time. Books the appointment. Takes the enquiry. Sends the confirmation. No missed calls. No lost bookings. DM me if you want to see how it works."

**Delivery notes:**
- First line: slow and clean. This is the hook.
- "20 and 40 percent" — pause slightly before saying the number. Let it register.
- Keep it factual. No hype. The numbers do the work.

0:00–0:05

## REEL 4 — The no-show problem (dental / clinic angle)
**Hook:** "A dental practice with 10 no-shows a week is losing around PS3,000 a month. Every month."

**FULL SCRIPT:**

0:00–0:05
"A dental practice with 10 no-shows a week is losing around PS3,000 a month. Every month."

0:05–0:35
"The fix is not complicated. Most no-shows happen because patients forget, or because the appointment feels too far away to matter yet. An automated reminder system sends a message 48 hours before, and again 24 hours before. Patients confirm or reschedule. The slot stays filled. We've seen practices go from 10 no-shows a week to 2. That's a significant chunk of revenue recovered with zero extra staff. If you run a clinic or practice and this sounds familiar, DM me."

**Delivery notes:**
- Open with the number. Say it clearly and let it sit.
- "Zero extra staff" — this is the thing that makes it feel low-risk. Emphasise it.
- Target audience is practice owners. Speak to them directly, not to a general audience.

0:00–0:05
"The businesses that adopt AI systems in the next 12 months will have an advantage that's very hard to close."

0:05–0:35
"Not because AI is magic. Because their competitors are still doing everything manually. Every missed call is being answered. Every customer is being followed up.
