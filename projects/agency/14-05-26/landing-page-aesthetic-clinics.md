## PROBLEM SECTION

### H2
A missed aesthetic call is a £150 to £350 booking handed to your competitor.

### Body

**The data on missed calls in UK aesthetics:**

- UK aesthetic clinics miss **30 to 42% of inbound calls**, with an average baseline missed-call rate of 29% ([Xtreme Gen AI, 2024](https://xtremegenai.com/blogs/missed-calls-costing-high-value-clinics-usa-uk))
- **85% of patients will not call back** after their first attempt is unanswered ([same source](https://xtremegenai.com/blogs/missed-calls-costing-high-value-clinics-usa-uk))
- **67% will contact a competitor** if their first-choice clinic does not answer quickly ([same source](https://xtremegenai.com/blogs/missed-calls-costing-high-value-clinics-usa-uk))
- In a 14-clinic European cohort, **34% of daily call volume arrives between 12:00 and 14:00**, when answer rates drop to 41% ([same source](https://xtremegenai.com/blogs/missed-calls-costing-high-value-clinics-usa-uk))

**What that means in pounds:**

- Average Botox treatment cost in the UK: **£150 to £350** ([Apex Aesthetics, 2026](https://www.apexaesthetics.co.uk/guides/botox-cost-uk-2026))
- Single converted aesthetics patient lifetime value: **£2,950 to £7,250 per year**, **£15,000+ over two to three years** ([Boutique SEO](https://boutiqueseo.co.uk/the-real-lifetime-value-of-an-aesthetics-patient-and-why-seo-is-worth-it/))
- Calculated revenue loss for an average clinic: **£23,000 to £31,000 per year** from missed calls alone ([Xtreme Gen AI](https://xtremegenai.com/blogs/missed-calls-costing-high-value-clinics-usa-uk))
- UK aesthetic clinic acquisition cost per patient: **£220 average, £35.71 at best-in-class clinics with 70% lead-to-sale conversion** ([Klatch, 2025](https://www.klatch.co.uk/blog/patient-acquisition-cost))

**The cancellation problem on top of missed calls:**

- Aesthetic clinics carry a **16% cancellation rate** and **5% no-show rate**, notably higher than beauty salons at 3.16-3.59% ([Professional Beauty UK](https://professionalbeauty.co.uk/beauty-appointment-cancellations-income-loss-survey))
- **56% of UK aesthetic businesses** say cancellations cause significant income loss ([same source](https://professionalbeauty.co.uk/beauty-appointment-cancellations-income-loss-survey))
- A clinic turning over £12,000 monthly loses approximately **£960 per month** to no-shows and late cancellations ([same source](https://professionalbeauty.co.uk/beauty-appointment-cancellations-income-loss-survey))

**The operational reality:**

Your reception team handles the room, the consultation diary, the EMR notes, the consent forms, the deposits, the prescriber coordination, the recall, and the social DMs. The phone rings while they are doing all of that. Hiring a second receptionist solves one shift. Click solves all of it, 24 hours a day.

## REGULATORY FRAMING (must remain on the page)

### H2
What the AI does. And what it does not.

### Body

Aesthetic practice is regulated. Click is built to respect that.

**The AI does:**
- Answer every inbound call 24/7
- Book consultations into your software
- Send confirmations, reminders, and rebooking requests
- Capture enquiry details and route to your team
- Run automated recall sequences for follow-up appointments

**The AI does not:**
- Provide medical advice or treatment recommendations
- Make clinical claims about outcomes
- Promise prescribing decisions
- Replace the prescriber consultation

Botulinum toxin is a Prescription-Only Medicine. Only registered prescribers (doctors, dentists, independent-prescribing nurses, pharmacists) may prescribe it. From 1 June 2025, all NMC nursing prescribers must conduct face-to-face consultations before prescribing non-surgical cosmetic medicines ([source: Aesthetics Associates, 2025](https://www.aestheticsassociates.com/2025/04/29/remote-prescribing-in-aesthetics/)).

The AI's role ends at booking the consultation. The prescriber's role begins there. We build the agent so the line is visible to every caller.

**On data:**
- All call recordings stored on UK-based servers
- GDPR-aligned data handling
- Data Processing Agreement signed before any patient data is collected
- Clinic-controlled call recording policy (you decide what is stored)

## FAQ

### H2
Common questions from clinic owners and practice managers.

**Will the AI provide medical advice or clinical recommendations?**
No. The agent is trained to handle administrative questions and booking. Anything clinical (treatment suitability, dosing, post-procedure concerns) is routed to your prescriber immediately. We script the regulatory boundaries into the agent.

**Is patient data handled compliantly?**
Yes. We are UK GDPR aligned. Call recordings stored on UK-based servers. Data Processing Agreement signed before any patient data is collected. Clinic-controlled retention policies.

**Will it integrate with Pabau / Phorest Medical / ANS / Consentz?**
Yes for all four, plus Clinicminds. Bookings made by the voice agent sync straight into your existing system.

**Can the AI handle treatment-specific questions (e.g., "Is Botox right for me?")**
The agent explains general treatment information (what Botox is, what fillers are, what a consultation involves) but does not assess suitability or recommend specific treatments. Those conversations route to your prescriber during the in-person consultation.

**What about prescriber rules and face-to-face consultations?**
The agent books the consultation. It does not pretend to replace it. Per NMC requirements from 1 June 2025, all prescribers must conduct face-to-face consultations before prescribing injectables. The agent's job ends when the consultation is scheduled.

**How does the agent handle aesthetic emergencies or complications?**
The agent identifies complication keywords (swelling, vascular events, pain post-treatment, allergic reaction) and routes the caller immediately to your designated emergency line or on-call clinician. You define the protocol during onboarding.

**Can callers stay anonymous if they want to?**
Yes. Aesthetic enquiries are often discreet. The agent can take an enquiry, send relevant information by WhatsApp/SMS/email, and only request identifying details when the caller is ready to book.

**What happens if patients don't like talking to an AI?**
The agent introduces itself as your clinic's "AI assistant" and offers to transfer to a human at any point. We track patient sentiment in the first 90 days and adjust if callers consistently push back.

**Can I cancel?**
30 days written notice, monthly rolling. We deactivate the system and hand over all patient communication templates at no extra charge.
