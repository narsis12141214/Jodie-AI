## PROBLEM SECTION

### H2
The calls you're missing are the bookings you're losing.

### Body
A typical London restaurant takes 50 to 100 booking calls a week. Between service hours, prep, and the moments when your team is on the floor, somewhere between a third and half of those calls go to voicemail. Most callers don't leave a message. They call the next restaurant on the list.

The cost adds up faster than most owners realise. At an average booking value of £105 for three covers, a single missed call on a Friday evening is gone revenue. Twenty missed calls a week at that average is £8,400 a month walking out the door before you've sat down to count it.

Voicemail doesn't fix this. Hiring a receptionist costs £1,500 to £1,800 a month for partial coverage. Sending bookings to a generic answering service strips out the warmth that makes your restaurant feel like yours.

Click Voice solves all three problems at once.

Every booking gets an instant confirmation in the channel the guest prefers. Day-of reminders go out automatically. No-shows drop. Walk-ins fill the gap.

## FAQ

### H2
Common questions from restaurant owners.

**How does it sound? Generic or like a real person?**
We use the latest natural-voice models and train the agent on your script, your tone, and your restaurant's name. Most callers don't realise it's AI. We can also tune the personality. Warm and chatty for an Italian neighbourhood spot. Crisp and professional for a fine-dining room. Your call.

**What if my menu or hours change?**
You send us a WhatsApp, and we update the agent the same day. No technical fiddling on your side.

**Can it transfer calls to me when needed?**
Yes. We set up warm-transfer triggers based on your rules. Press requests, group bookings over 12, anything you flag as "always escalate" gets routed directly to you or your team.

**What if it gets a booking wrong?**
You see every booking in the dashboard with the call recording attached. If there's a problem, we adjust the agent the same day. Mistakes in the first 14 days are common as we tune; that's why the trial exists.

**How does this compare to OpenTable's voice agent?**
OpenTable will offer a generic voice agent as part of its platform. It will work for some restaurants. Click Voice is built for yours specifically, sounds like yours, updates in an hour when you need it. They give you a feature. We give you a team behind it.

**What if my booking system isn't on this list?**
Tell us what you use. If we can integrate, we will. If we can't, we route bookings into a Google Calendar your team already uses.

**Can I upgrade later?**
Yes. Most clients start with Click Voice and move to Click+ within 60 to 90 days, once they've seen the voice agent work and want us to also run their social media, content, and website. We offer 50% off the first month of Click+ when you upgrade within 90 days.
