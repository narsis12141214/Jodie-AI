We integrate with all three. Bookings made by the voice agent sync straight into your existing system.

For bookings: yes, comprehensively. For takeaway and delivery orders: we can build it for fixed-menu operations, but full ordering systems typically belong on Click+ or higher.

Not directly. Those platforms have their own ordering flows. We focus on your inbound voice, WhatsApp, and social channels.

How does the agent sound? Generic or like a real person?

We use natural-voice models and train the agent on your script, your tone, and your venue's name. Most callers don't realise it's AI. We can tune the personality. Warm and chatty for a neighbourhood cafe. Crisp and professional for a fine-dining room. Your call.
