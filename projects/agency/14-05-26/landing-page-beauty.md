Weekly Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok posts. Carousels, reels, and stories that show your work. Review requests after every appointment. Response management on every comment and DM.

## FAQ

### H2
Common questions from salon owners.

**Does it integrate with Phorest / Fresha / Treatwell / Acuity?**
Yes for all four. Bookings made by the voice agent sync straight into your existing system.

**Will the agent take a deposit at booking?**
Yes, if your booking policy requires it. We integrate with Stripe or your existing deposit flow.

**Can it handle WhatsApp Business?**
Yes. We register your WhatsApp Business account (if you don't have one already) and submit message templates to Meta for approval as part of setup.

**What about treatments where consultation is required first?**
We script the agent to capture the booking enquiry, gather the relevant details (medical history flags, previous treatments, photos if your protocol requires), and route to your team for the consultation booking specifically.

**Will the AI compete with my existing salon software?**
No. We sit on top of it. Your salon software stays as the source of truth. The agent feeds bookings into it.

**Can I cancel?**
30 days written notice, monthly rolling. We hand over all content and templates at no extra charge.
