Common questions from practice managers and owners.

Is patient data handled securely?

Yes. We are UK GDPR compliant. All call recordings and patient data are stored on UK-based servers. We sign a Data Processing Agreement as standard before any patient data is collected.

Does the AI handle clinical questions?

The agent is trained to handle administrative and basic informational questions. Anything clinical (treatment recommendations, post-op concerns, dental emergencies) is escalated to a clinician immediately via your preferred channel.

Will it integrate with my practice management system?

We integrate with Software of Excellence (SOE), Dentally, EXACT (where APIs allow), and most modern booking systems. For systems without integration, we run bookings into a shared calendar your team uses, which syncs back to your system manually or via Zapier.

How does the recall work?

We pull your recall list from your practice management system (or you upload it). The agent runs an automated sequence: WhatsApp first, SMS second, voice call third. Patients who respond get rebooked into available slots. Patients who don't are flagged for your front desk to review.

What about dental emergencies?

The agent identifies emergency keywords (pain, swelling, trauma) and routes the caller immediately to your designated emergency line or on-call dentist. You define the protocol during onboarding.

Can you handle calls in languages other than English?

The agent supports multiple languages on Click Pro and Click Elite tiers. Click+ is English-only by default but can be customised.

What happens if patients don't like talking to an AI?

The agent introduces itself as your practice's "AI assistant" and offers to transfer to a human at any point. Patient satisfaction in the first 90 days is the metric we track most closely. If callers consistently push back, we adjust the script and tone.

Can I cancel?

30 days written notice, monthly rolling. We deactivate the system at the end of the notice period and hand over all your patient communication templates and content at no extra charge.
