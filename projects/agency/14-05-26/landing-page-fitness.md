## PROBLEM SECTION

### H2
The leads come in at 9pm. Your desk closes at 6pm.

### Body
Three problems sit on every gym owner's desk:

**1. Lead leakage outside hours.**
Most gym enquiries arrive evenings and weekends, the times your front desk isn't staffed. Voicemail and unanswered DMs lose 40 to 60% of these leads. The prospect calls the gym down the road and joins them instead.

**2. Member retention slips below the line.**
Industry average annual retention is 66.4%. The 33.6% who leave each year are the difference between a profitable gym and a marginal one. Research shows just 2 staff-member interactions per month reduces cancellation risk by 33% ([GymMaster](https://www.gymmaster.com/blog/gym-membership-and-retention-statistics/)). The problem: those interactions don't happen because no one has time.

**3. Class bookings and waitlists clog the front desk.**
Every "is the 6pm available?" call eats reception time. Every "I need to cancel my booking" message sits in a DM queue until someone clears it. Multiply by 200 members.

Click runs the AI layer that absorbs all three jobs.

## SOLUTION SECTION

### H2
A 24/7 desk for your gym, without hiring anyone.

### Three pillars

**1. AI voice agent that captures every lead.**
Trained on your membership tiers, your class schedule, your trial offer, your trainer roster. Handles new-member enquiries, books trial sessions, answers pricing and timetable questions, captures contact details for follow-up.

**2. Class bookings, waitlists, and member messages on autopilot.**
Members book classes via voice or WhatsApp. Waitlists fill automatically when someone cancels. Reminders go out 24 hours and 2 hours before class. The front desk only sees the messages that needed human attention.

**3. Retention sequences that keep members.**
Automated check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days. Win-back sequences for lapsed members. Birthday and milestone messages. The "human" touch that retention demands, run by AI, monitored by us.

## FAQ

### H2
Common questions from gym and studio owners.

**Does it integrate with Mindbody / Glofox / TeamUp / Acuity?**
Yes for all four where APIs allow. Bookings made by the voice agent sync straight into your existing system.

**Can it handle membership sales, or just bookings?**
The agent qualifies new-member enquiries, books trials, takes deposits where required. Final membership signups can be closed by the agent (where your system supports automated sign-up) or handed warm to your sales team. We design this with you in discovery.

**What about complex pricing (founder rates, corporate plans, etc.)?**
We script the agent to handle your full pricing matrix or escalate complex pricing conversations to your team based on rules you set.

**Will the agent learn class names and trainer schedules?**
Yes. We update the agent the same day you change your timetable.

**What if a member wants to cancel their membership?**
The agent captures the cancellation request, runs a brief retention conversation (if you want one in the flow), and routes to your team for final processing. You don't get blindsided by cancellations.

**Can I cancel the service?**
30 days written notice, monthly rolling. We hand over all content, templates, and member communication sequences at no extra charge.
