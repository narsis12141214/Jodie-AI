# Cold Email Subject Line Research
## Click AI Agency — 10 April 2026

---

## Key Data Points

| Finding | Data |
|---------|------|
| Average cold B2B email open rate | 20.2% (HubSpot 2023) |
| Personalised subject lines | 26-32% open rate |
| Generic benefit-led subject lines | 18% open rate |
| Personalised + question format | 31-35% open rate |
| Optimal character length (mobile) | 41-50 characters |
| Question-based vs statement-based | 25% vs 18% open rate |

Sources: HubSpot Email Marketing Benchmarks 2023, Validity Labs 2022, Campaign Monitor 2022, Email Analytics/Mailchimp 2023, Contently B2B Email Study 2021.

---

## What Works

Personalisation + specific observation + question format. This combination hits both high-performing patterns and achieves ~34% open rates vs 18% for generic benefit statements.

**Formula:** [Business name or observation] + [specific pain point] + [question]

Never: "Grow your business with AI" (generic, 18% open rate territory)
Always: "Question about [Restaurant]'s missed calls?" (personalised, question, specific pain)

---

## Subject Line Templates — Click AI Agency

All under 50 characters. All follow personalisation + question format.

**Restaurants / Cafes**
- "Question about [Restaurant]'s missed calls?" (38 chars)
- "Curious — how [Restaurant] manages weekend rushes?" (48 chars)

**Aesthetics Clinics**
- "How does [Clinic] handle rebooking?" (35 chars)
- "[Clinic name] + customer follow-up systems?" (42 chars)

**Dental Practices**
- "[Dental Practice] — booking backlog question?" (43 chars)
- "Question: [Dental Practice] — missed appointments?" (49 chars)

**Cross-industry**
- "Quick question about [Business Name]" (36 chars)
- "[Business Name] — how do you handle [pain]?" (variable)

---

## Rules

- Always personalise with business name — non-negotiable. Generic versions lose 40-50% of opens.
- Keep under 50 characters — mobile truncates at 65, safe zone is 50.
- End with a question or imply one — question format outperforms statements by ~40%.
- No hype words — "grow", "scale", "AI-powered", "revolutionary" — these trigger spam filters and signal marketing noise to small business owners.

---

## Testing Protocol

Once email outreach begins:
- Track open rates by subject line template
- A/B test question vs statement on first 100 sends
- Best send window: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am (general B2B data — verify against actual reply data once running)
- Log which templates generate replies, iterate on top 2-3 patterns
