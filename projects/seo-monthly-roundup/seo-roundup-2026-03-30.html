Executive Summary

  • Three keywords are on the edge of page 1 and need content pushes now: "couples photoshoot london" (pos 7.1, page 1!), "elopement photographer london" (pos 10.6), and "london couple photoshoot" (pos 12.1). These pages need H1 optimisation, content depth, and internal linking to lock positions.
  • Micro wedding is the single biggest missing page. Beatrici, Lex Fleming, Diana V, Alina Pullen, Matilda Delves, and Alexei Wells all have dedicated micro wedding pages. Hadi has none. This is a high-conversion keyword cluster with growing demand and no current coverage.
  • The proposal photographer niche is your clearest content moat opportunity. Matt Badenoch (300+ proposals, £700 package page), The Now Time (6+ proposal pages), and Adams dominate. Hadi has one blog post. A 2,000+ word dedicated proposal photographer page with pricing, process, and FAQs would compete immediately.
  • Alexei Wells remains the most structured SEO threat — individual pages for North, South, East, and West London plus civil wedding, micro wedding, and elopement. His location-specific architecture captures high-intent local searches that Hadi is entirely absent from.
  • Showit's JS-heavy rendering is the single biggest technical blocker. Content is embedded in JSON, not semantic HTML. Google is struggling to index pages fully. Missing meta descriptions on every major service page compounds this. These are urgent fixes.
  • The homepage title tag is wrong: "London Elopement Photographer | Hadi Photography" — but the homepage should target broader brand + couples queries. This is depressing CTR on 24,500 impressions per 6 months across non-elopement searches.
  • YouTube is completely open. No London couples or elopement photographer has a dominant channel. Proposal reaction videos and elopement day films are the highest-potential entry points with zero meaningful competition currently.

Full Site Audit

Pages discovered via Google site search and Search Console. Site runs on Showit — a JS-heavy page builder that embeds content in JSON, creating significant crawlability challenges. This is the root cause of many SEO issues.

Critical Technical Issues

  • CRITICAL Showit platform — content in JSON, not HTML. Google has to render JavaScript to read page content. Many pages show no semantic H2/H3 structure from the crawler's perspective. This is suppressing all rankings.
  • CRITICAL Missing meta descriptions on all major service pages. Homepage, engagement page, elopement photographer page, elopement packages — all confirmed missing. Reduces CTR by estimated 20-30%.
  • CRITICAL Duplicate elopement pages. /london-elopement-packages (pos 63, 2,797 impressions) and /elopement-wedding-packages (pos 52, 3,161 impressions) are splitting authority. Consolidate into one page with a 301 redirect.
  • URGENT Homepage title tag mismatch. Title: "London Elopement Photographer | Hadi Photography" — but the homepage is showing for broad queries like "london photographer", "couple photoshoot london", "wedding photographer london". Homepage title should be broader.
  • URGENT H1 tags are not keyword-targeted. Engagement page H1: "Capturing your timeless love in London's secret corners" — not "London Engagement Photographer". Google uses H1 as a strong relevancy signal.
  • WARNING Cart/shop URLs indexed. /cart/?remove_item= appearing in Search Console. All shop, cart, and checkout URLs need noindex.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Pre-wedding keywords already on page 1: "pre wedding photographer london" (pos 5.3), "pre wedding photoshoot london" (pos 6.5), "couples photoshoot london" (pos 7.1). Proof that the site CAN rank — it just needs the CTR fixes.
  • Homepage gets 24,500+ impressions/6mo — strong domain for its age. The authority is there. It's being wasted on a narrow title tag.
  • Elopement content depth: /london-elopement-photographer page runs 8,000-12,000 words with strong narrative. Competitors like Dani Elizabeth have thinner pages.
  • Surprise proposal blog post exists and is indexed — a foundation to build the full service page on top of.
  • Engagement locations blog (1,980 impressions) — has traction and just needs a CTR fix to drive real traffic.

Weaknesses

  • Showit platform: JS-heavy rendering means Google struggles to read content. This is the single biggest technical drag on all rankings.
  • No meta descriptions on any major service page. Google is writing its own — and they're weak.
  • Duplicate elopement pages splitting authority across two URLs with combined 5,958 impressions and 8 clicks.
  • Off-brand pages (fashion, lightroom presets, shop) confusing Google about site niche. Dragging E-E-A-T down.
  • H1 tags not keyword-targeted — brand language in H1 ("Capturing your timeless love") instead of keyword phrase ("London Engagement Photographer").
  • No pricing transparency on proposal or engagement pages — Matt Badenoch publishes £700 proposal package upfront. Couples scan for price before enquiring.

Opportunities

  • Micro wedding page: Zero current coverage. 6 competitors have dedicated pages. High-conversion intent. Create this page now.
  • Proposal photographer service page: Currently only a blog post. Matt Badenoch built a content moat with 300+ proposals cited. A proper page with pricing, FAQs, and gallery can rank within weeks.
  • CTR fix on page-1 keywords: "pre wedding photographer london" gets only 1 click from pos 5.3 on 321 impressions. Adding a meta description alone could generate 10-15x more clicks from existing rankings.
  • YouTube first-mover: No London couples/elopement photographer has a dominant channel. Proposal reaction BTS videos and elopement day films have zero meaningful competition.
  • Engagement locations page upgrade: 1,980 impressions at pos 19 with 1 click. Rewrite title, add meta description, add H2 structure — could be pulling 50+ clicks/month.
  • Pre-wedding photoshoot page (currently ranking but no dedicated page) — build it and these pos 5-6 keywords solidify.

Threats

  • Alexei Wells has the most dangerous SEO architecture: location-specific pages for North, South, East, and West London, plus civil wedding, micro wedding, and elopement. Each page targets a specific high-intent search. This structure compounds over time.
  • Olly Knight won UK Wedding Photographer of the Year 2026 — award pages and credibility signals now working against Hadi on "best london wedding photographer" searches.
  • The Now Time is building a proposal content cluster: proposal ideas, packages, planner, locations, summer guide, reaction video blog posts. This is what a content moat looks like.
  • Alina Pullen is published in Vogue — press mentions create inbound links and E-E-A-T signals that are hard to overcome without media coverage of your own.
  • Jake Timms holds "Best Elopement Wedding Photographer 2023" — award-backed elopement authority directly competing with Hadi's primary positioning.
  • Miss Gen and Charlie Britz both have explicit "award-winning" positioning across multiple search results — Hadi has no award or press mention visible in any search result snippets.
