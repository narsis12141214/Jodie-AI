1. Executive Summary

Five things that matter most this month:
  1. SEO is generating real enquiries. Two photography leads (Charlotte Adams, Maria Khan) came directly from Google in April. First concrete proof that SEO recovery is translating into business. India is the #2 country by clicks (11 clicks, 6.47% CTR), validating the international couples ICA.
  2. The engagement photographer page is the biggest hidden opportunity. /london-engagement-photographer has 1,734 impressions and 0 clicks at position 59. This page has massive search demand but is buried. Fixing this one page could generate 10-20 clicks per month.
  3. 64 total clicks across 800+ keywords in April. The site is being found for a wide range of relevant search terms. The challenge is conversion (0.63% overall CTR) and position (many keywords in 20-60 range).
  4. "Pre wedding photoshoot london" is already working. Blog #6 is at position 4.5 with 32 impressions and 1 click after just 3 weeks. This validates the blog strategy.
  5. "Micro wedding photographer london" already has 5 impressions at position 7.4 with no dedicated page. Blog #7 will capture this traffic immediately once published.
