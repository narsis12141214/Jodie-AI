LIVE RESEARCH — 25 DIRECT COMPETITORS

Executive Summary

  • Three pages are within striking distance of page 1 — couples photoshoot (pos 9.3), elopement photographer (pos 10.6), and pre-wedding photographer (pos 5.3, already there). These need content updates now to lock in top positions.
  • Micro wedding is the single biggest untapped gap. Matt Badenoch, Beatrici, Diana V, Lex Fleming, and Alexei Wells all have dedicated micro wedding pages. You have none. This keyword cluster has high conversion intent.
  • The proposal photographer niche is contested but winnable. Adams (400+ weddings), Matt Badenoch (300+ proposals), and Olly Knight (2026 UK Wedding Photographer of the Year) dominate. You have one blog post and no dedicated page. A proper 2,000+ word proposal page would compete immediately.
  • Alexei Wells is the most dangerous SEO threat — he has individual pages for North, South, East, and West London plus civil wedding, micro wedding, and elopement. His location-specific structure is capturing high-converting local searches you're entirely absent from.
  • The Now Time owns the proposal content cluster entirely with 6+ dedicated pages (ideas, packages, planner, locations, summer guide). This is the template for what a content moat looks like — and the gap you need to fill in elopement.
  • YouTube is completely open. No London couples or elopement photographer has a dominant channel. The format gap is real and actionable — proposal reaction videos and elopement day films are the highest-potential entry points.

SWOT Analysis

Data-backed from live research across 25 direct competitors and Google Search Console.

Strengths

  • "Couple photoshoot london" at pos 9.3 — one content update from page 1
  • "Elopement photographer london" at pos 10.6 — already in top 10
  • "Pre wedding photographer london" at pos 5.3 — page 1, low CTR to fix
  • Three strong 2026 guide posts freshly indexed and gaining traction
  • 500+ 5-star reviews claimed on homepage — strong social proof signal
  • LGBTQ-friendly explicitly stated — differentiator most competitors don't flag prominently
  • Elopement packages from £1,400 — competitive entry point vs. Alina Pullen (£1,800+)
  • Photography + full elopement planning service — broader value prop than most
  • Engagement locations page at pos 19 with 1,980 impressions — very close to page 1
  • Blog building momentum: 3 comprehensive 2026 guides published and indexed

Weaknesses

  • No micro wedding page — all five main competitors have dedicated pages
  • No dedicated surprise proposal photographer page — Adams, Badenoch, and Olly Knight own this
  • Engagement page at pos 47 with 5,120 impressions — near-zero CTR, needs full rewrite
  • Elopement packages page at pos 63 — 2,797 impressions but invisible on page 7
  • No location-specific pages — Alexei Wells has North, South, East, West London covered
  • No town hall specific pages — Miss Gen, Lex Fleming, Diana V all have Marylebone content
  • Average CTR of 0.64% — title tags and meta descriptions are not compelling enough
  • Lightroom preset blog post (off-brand, outdated) still indexed and diluting focus
  • Family photography and portrait pages — off-brand, diluting elopement/couples positioning
  • Site on Showit — heavy JS can limit semantic HTML structure crawlers see
  • No pricing transparency — competitors like Diana V (£750) and Beatrici (£480/hr) attract price-sensitive searchers

Opportunities

  • Micro wedding page: immediate gap, high intent, 5 competitors already ranking — create now
  • Surprise proposal photographer page: Adams has 400+ weddings but weak content depth — Hadi can compete with a thorough guide
  • Town hall elopement pages: Old Marylebone, Chelsea, Hackney, Islington — location specificity drives conversion
  • East/North London location pages — Beatrici explicitly targets Shoreditch/Hackney/Peckham and it works
  • "Best places to propose in London" long-form guide — Adams and Olly Knight rank for this, high-intent search
  • LLM visibility: content answering "Who are the best elopement photographers in London?" gets cited by AI — nobody is optimised for this yet
  • YouTube channel: no dominant London elopement/couples photographer channel exists — first mover advantage is real
  • Alinapullen's Guardian feature proves editorial coverage is achievable in this niche — pitch opportunity
  • Pre-wedding photography guide for international couples — Nick Rose is ranking for this; big opportunity for Hadi

Threats

  • Olly Knight won UK Wedding Photographer of the Year 2026 — authority + press coverage boost incoming
  • The Now Time owns the proposal content cluster with 6+ dedicated pages — extremely hard to displace
  • Alexei Wells has systematically built location pages for all of London — structural SEO advantage
  • Matt Badenoch has 300+ proposals + micro wedding + engagement all covered — strongest combined competitor
  • Diana V publishes pricing openly (£750 for 2hr) — attracting price-sensitive searchers away from Hadi
  • Alinapullen's Guardian feature creates editorial backlinks and credibility hard to replicate quickly
  • Miss Gen's press coverage (Love My Dress, Wedding Industry Awards finalist) signals long-term authority
  • 1 year of social dormancy means algorithm signals on Instagram are weak — rebuilding from scratch
  • Showit platform JS-heavy structure may limit how well Google crawls page content vs. competitors on simpler CMSes
