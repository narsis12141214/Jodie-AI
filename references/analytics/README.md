# Analytics Drop Zone

Drop your website analytics exports here. The idea mining skill reads the most recently modified file in this folder automatically.

## What to Export

**Google Analytics 4**
- Go to Reports > Engagement > Pages and screens
- Export top 20-30 pages by sessions as CSV
- Also useful: Traffic acquisition report (where visitors come from)

**Google Search Console** *(highly recommended)*
- Go to Performance > Search results
- Export your top queries and pages as CSV
- This shows what people are actually searching to find your site

## File Naming

Name doesn't matter. Use something like:
- `ga-export-2026-03.csv`
- `search-console-march-2026.csv`

## How Often to Update

Monthly is ideal. Before running the idea mining skill is a good habit.

## What the Skill Does With This Data

It reads your analytics to understand:
- Which pages/topics are already driving traffic (double down on these)
- Which services visitors look at most (content demand signals)
- Search queries bringing people in (keyword opportunities)

This context makes the generated ideas much more targeted than running without it.
