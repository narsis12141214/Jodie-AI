## Constraint Acknowledgements (read before the plan)

**Agency is primary.** Photography runs in parallel but does not compete for Hadi's peak hours. Photography tasks assigned to Hadi are scheduled for mornings before outreach or evenings. Jodie runs the SEO pipeline and content calendar — Hadi's photography involvement is filming, approving, and booking.

**Enquiry target is lagging.** 50 enquiries/month does not happen because of blog posts alone. It requires: keyword rankings (takes 4-8 weeks from publish to rank), a pricing page that converts, an enquiry flow that works, and enough Instagram authority to confirm the decision. All four must move simultaneously.

**Instagram growth target is aggressive.** 20,000 followers from 13,500 in 60 days requires ~108 followers/day net. That requires daily posting and at least 3 Reels per week. This is achievable — but only if Hadi is consistent and on camera regularly. Consistency beats quality here.

## Risk Register

**Risk 1: Blog posting falls below one per week**
Mitigation: Jodie owns the pipeline from brief through Final Clearance. Hadi's only required action is publishing in WordPress (15 minutes) and approving the HTML. If Hadi is unavailable, posts queue until he can publish — they do not get cancelled. A consistent 1/week schedule is more important than occasionally posting 2.

**Risk 2: Instagram posting becomes inconsistent**
Mitigation: Jodie schedules every post in Metricool in advance. The only thing that requires Hadi is Reels filming. A week without filmed content falls back to static images and carousels — which maintains the daily posting habit even if Reel frequency drops temporarily.

**Risk 3: SEO recovery is slower than expected**
Mitigation: Blog posts from March are already indexed and showing early position movement. The recovery is confirmed — the question is speed. If positions are not moving by day 30 for the most recent posts, run a technical audit before assuming the content is the problem. Indexing delays, internal link gaps, and page speed are faster fixes than rewriting content.
